ABOUT

We get it, you're awkward. Luckily for you so are we! Welcome to Awkward Party Pal. You may be that person who thrives on the internet, but in real life you're...well let's not even go there. We both know you resemble the dude with the party hat. Instead of showing up to an event empty handed (if you actually ever decide to leave your house) we built this website to generate conversation starters so you'll never have to deal with awkward silence again!

BE AN EXTROVERT!

Jk, jk. We’d never ask you to do anything that crazy. So how can Awkward Party Pal help you become the life of the party? It starts with a little RECON. See that form there? Use that to start your party prep. Put your internet stalking skills to good use and start making notes about partygoers on the RSVP list. No, not creepy notes. Just the fun stuff, and maybe some things you’d be smart to avoid. We’ll keep track of your notes, and when it’s time to party, you can use us as your own personal party assistant.
